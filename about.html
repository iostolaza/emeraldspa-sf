Our Mission

At Emerald Spa, we believe that everyone has wellness within them, and it is our passion to guide every guest to their optimal state of well-being. We know that true wellness starts from within the body, and we deliver authentic, results-oriented facial treatments and holistic body practices to detoxify, release, and nourish the mind, body, and spirit.

Our Approach

We pursue a holistic approach that addresses and treats the underlying sources of stress caused by the demands of urban life. Rather than quick fixes, we treat the whole you. We have dedicated specialists that can help you unleash your own personal wellness from within through advanced skincare, aromatherapy, and profound relaxation.

Our Staff

Our staff is expert in each of their specific service areas and understands how targeted treatments work best to contribute to your overall health, well-being, and feelings of contentment. Our certified practitioners are here to help, heal, and give you the education and understanding you need to make self-care part of your everyday regimen.

Our Setting

Our sanctuary offers you a beautiful, tranquil getaway. Located centrally in downtown San Francisco, this is a private place to relax, unwind, and decompress. Escaping the noise of the city the moment you walk through our doors is central to our philosophy and offering.

Your Privacy, Our Priority

Discretion is at the heart of everything we do. Every guest is welcomed into a calm, confidential environment where your comfort and privacy come first. We never share your personal information, and your visit always stays between you and our team. From the moment you arrive, you can relax knowing you are in a safe, respectful, and completely private space.

Our Promise

Our mission and goal is simple: to meet and exceed your expectations with every single visit. We hold ourselves to the highest standard of service, taking the time to understand what you need and going the extra step to make sure you leave feeling cared for, restored, and genuinely valued. Your satisfaction is the measure we hold ourselves to.

Privacy Policy

Updated January 1, 2026

Emerald Spa values its users' privacy. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect and use personal information from those who visit our website or make use of our online facilities and services, and what we will and will not do with the information we collect.

Information We Collect

It is always up to you whether to disclose personally identifiable information to us. We only collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and phone number when you call to schedule an appointment. We collect this data solely to better understand your needs, improve our services, and contact you regarding your appointments. We do not use any third-party booking platforms.

Use & Disclosure of Information

Emerald Spa does not now, nor will it in the future, sell, rent or lease any of its customer lists and/or names to any third parties. We may disclose your information only as necessary to provide services you have ordered, or as required by law.
